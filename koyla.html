About the Brand

The Koyla
Story

Koyla was born from a belief that great food should never be out of reach. Under the NIVPL umbrella, Koyla brings the bold, smoky depth of North Indian cuisine — charcoal-kissed kebabs, flavour-packed gravies, and hearty portions — to everyday dining.

The name itself speaks to the brand's soul: koyla, meaning coal, represents the raw, honest energy of cooking over fire. Every dish at Koyla carries that spirit — unpolished, bold, and deeply satisfying.

Koyla is proof that accessible pricing and exceptional taste are not in conflict. They are, in fact, perfectly compatible — when quality and discipline are baked into every step of the process.
