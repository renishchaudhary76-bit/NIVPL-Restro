About the Brand

The Assal
Story

Assal Mawla Darbar is a deep bow to the rich culinary heritage of Kolhapur — a cuisine known for its intensity, its generosity, and its proud regional identity. Created to bring traditional Kolhapuri thalis to guests who know that real food has history.

The menu centres on the full Kolhapuri thali experience — fiery mutton rassa, tambda and pandhra rassa, bhakri, rice, and the kind of spread that feels like a celebration even on an ordinary Tuesday. Everything is cooked the traditional way: slow, careful, and with genuine respect for the recipe.

Assal Mawla Darbar does not soften the edges of Kolhapuri cuisine. It serves it as it is — bold, proud, and exactly as it should be.
