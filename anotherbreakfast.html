About the Brand

The Another
Story

Another Breakfast was built for people who believe the best meal of the day should be available all day. A modern café-style concept under NIVPL, it brings together the comfort of a hearty breakfast with the ease of a neighbourhood café.

The menu is fresh, approachable, and deeply satisfying — eggs in every form, toasts, pancakes, fresh juices, and warming beverages that make you want to linger. Everything is prepared with care using quality ingredients.

Another Breakfast is not just a meal — it is a moment. A pause in your day. A table that welcomes you at 7am and again at 3pm, with the same warmth and consistency every single time.
