Our Story

What began as a humble restaurant in Belagavi has grown into one of the region's most respected names in hospitality.

N I Ventures Private Limited (NIVPL) is the corporate home to the Niyaaz Group of Restaurants — a collection of distinct brands built on a shared foundation of taste, trust, and timeless quality.

From that first kitchen on P.B. Road, we have grown into a multi-brand enterprise spanning dine-in, delivery, and takeaway concepts across six cities. Yet the essence remains unchanged — creating food experiences that bring people together.

Every brand under NIVPL carries forward the same passion for authenticity, consistency, and warmth that started it all. Driven by vision, rooted in values, and powered by people who truly care.

Our Journey

Three and a half decades
of flavour

From a single kitchen to a multi-brand legacy

1989
The Beginning

On 20th October, the first Niyaaz Restaurant opened its doors on P.B. Road, Belagavi — a humble kitchen with one goal: serve good food at a fair price.

2010s
Corporate Identity

N I Ventures Private Limited was formally established, creating a unified corporate identity to house the group's growing portfolio of brands.

2000s
Brand Expansion

NIVPL began growing beyond its flagship outlet — launching new concepts across dine-in, delivery, and takeaway formats to serve diverse food needs.

Today
A Thriving Legacy

15 kitchens. 6 cities. 3 states. 600+ team members. ~95,000 monthly orders. Still driven by the same day-one passion for food that people trust.
