About the Brand

The Galangal
Story

Galangal is NIVPL's answer to the growing appetite for Asian cuisine in the region. Named after the aromatic root central to Southeast Asian cooking, Galangal brings together Chinese, Thai, and Japanese-inspired cuisine under one focused, carefully curated menu.

Every dish is crafted with attention to authenticity and balance — the umami depth of Asian broths, the brightness of fresh herbs, the satisfying complexity of wok-tossed preparations. Focused menus mean every item earns its place.

Galangal is for those who crave something different — a dining experience that travels, without leaving the city. Precise, flavourful, and consistently excellent.
