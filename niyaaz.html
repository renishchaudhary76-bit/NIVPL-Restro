About the Brand

The Niyaaz
Story

Niyaaz is where it all began. Founded in Belagavi on 20th October 1989, Niyaaz was born from a simple, powerful belief: that good food, served honestly, at a fair price, is something everyone deserves.

Over three and a half decades, Niyaaz has grown from a single restaurant on P.B. Road into a brand with multiple locations across Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Goa. The menu spans the full breadth of Awadhi and Mughlai cuisine — dum biryanis, slow-cooked curries, charcoal-grilled kebabs, rich kormas — all made with ingredients sourced fresh and recipes refined across generations.

To eat at Niyaaz is to experience a tradition that has never compromised on taste or trust. That is the Niyaaz promise — today, and for the decades to come.
