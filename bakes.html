About the Brand

The Bakes
Story

Bakes by Niyaaz is NIVPL's dedicated bakery brand — everything baked fresh daily using quality ingredients, with the same discipline that governs every kitchen in the group.

The range covers the everyday and the celebratory — fresh breads, buttery pastries, rich cakes, and seasonal specials. Whether a simple loaf for Sunday morning or a custom celebration cake, Bakes by Niyaaz delivers with care and consistency.

A bakery is one of the most honest expressions of food — there is nowhere to hide when ingredients and technique are everything. Bakes by Niyaaz embraces that challenge and rises to it every single morning.
